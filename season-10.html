            # Season 10

            The tenth season of Friends aired from September 25, 2003 to May 6, 2004.

            Precious is dumped by Mike, with Phoebe's help, on her Birthday.

            The Office S06E09 - Double Date

            Michael dumps Helene, Pam's mom, also on her Birthday.

            Monica got stuck with the cornrows while dancing around and singing "No, woman, no cry" from Bob Marley.

            The Office S09E07 - The Whale

            The music is also referenced by Erin, while she's sad about Andy sail trip to Bahamas to sell his family's boat.

            But now, boss is just slang for jerk in charge.
